# SAMCO-s-2019-Performance-Analysis-Report
Purpose of the Analysis

The SAMCO Supermarket Sales Dashboard was developed to provide a comprehensive, interactive overview of sales performance across time, regions, cities, and sales personnel.
Its primary objectives are to:

Monitor overall revenue performance
Identify regional and seasonal sales trends
Evaluate salesperson productivity
Support data-driven strategic decision-making

 Target Users
This dashboard is designed for:
Senior Management
Sales Managers
Regional Managers
Business Analysts
Operations and Strategy Teams

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Revenue
Total sales revenue generated during the year
Total Orders Raised
Number of customer orders processed
Total Shipping Fee
Total revenue generated from shipping fees
Monthly Revenue
Revenue trend across the 12 months
Revenue by Region
Sales performance across North, East, South, and West
Quantity by Region
Sales volume distribution by region
Revenue by Salesperson
Individual contribution to total revenue

Key Insights Summary
SAMCO generated 435K in annual revenue from 369 orders
Revenue peaks occur in June and December, indicating seasonal demand
The North region dominates revenue, despite balanced sales volumes
Sales productivity varies significantly across sales personnel
Shipping fees form a meaningful portion of total revenue

Business Implications
Seasonal demand requires proactive inventory and staffing planning
Regional pricing or product strategies may need optimization
Heavy reliance on top performers introduces performance risk
Growth opportunities exist in underperforming regions and cities
Recommendations
Leverage Peak Seasons
Plan promotions, inventory, and staffing around June and December peaks.
Replicate Top Sales Strategies
Document best practices from high-performing salespeople and scale across teams.
Optimize Regional Strategy
Review pricing and product mix in lower-revenue regions.
Strengthen Secondary Markets
Use targeted marketing to grow revenue outside major urban hubs.
Monitor Shipping Fee Impact
Test free-shipping thresholds or bundled offers to increase order value.
Limitations
Data reflects only one calendar year
Customer demographics and product-level details are not included
External factors (promotions, economic conditions) are not captured
Conclusion
The SAMCO Sales Dashboard successfully transforms raw transaction data into actionable insights. By aligning sales strategy, regional focus, and performance management with these findings, SAMCO can enhance revenue growth and operational efficiency.
